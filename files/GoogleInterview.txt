This task involves preparing for a technical interview with Google. The preparation includes studying data structures and algorithms, reviewing common coding interview questions, and practicing on platforms like LeetCode and HackerRank.

Tasks to be done:
1. Revise key algorithms: sorting, searching, dynamic programming.
2. Solve at least 10 problems on LeetCode (easy to medium difficulty).
3. Review Google interview-specific questions and solutions.
4. Prepare behavioral interview questions and practice answering them.

The goal is to feel confident and prepared for the interview within a week.
